How to play

Welcome to unReal Estate, the game where you become a real estate mogul! The goal of the game is to become a billionare (the richer the better) before January 2100. Your path of glory begins in december 1999 when you have saved enough dough to buy an apartment in Athens. You pay 20% of the cost and the bank will lend you the rest. This property will provide a rent in return and you will also be paying mortgage until you repay the debt. Beware of economic cycles as real estate will go up and down periodically. The game will last 10-20 minutes.

Actions you can perform:

  • Once you have enough savings buy an apartment to a max of 15 total.
  • After 1 year of owning a property you can sell it back to the market.
  • If you have enough savings, cancel your mortgage to avoid paying hefty interests.
  • Every 15 years you will be able to refurbish your property to avoid loosing value.
